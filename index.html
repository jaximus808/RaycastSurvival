Hue Gunner
Made by: Jaxon Poentis
How to Play

To move use the key combination WASD, W - Forward, A - Left, S - Back, D - Right. The number 1 key is to pause and unpause the game, To move the camera use the mouse or the left and right arrow keys. To shoot use the left mouse button or click space.

The goal of the game is too simply kill the most bad guys you can and to get powerups to make the expeierence easier using the points as currency. Score is also determined by how short the wave lasts incentivising the player to not take so long.
